A High School Hacking Competition

By High School Students

Capture the CTF (CTCTF) is a CTF-style competition for high school students inspired by PicoCTF, HSCTF, and EasyCTF. With combined efforts from HSCS, CTCTF will provide a fun and competitive environment.

For Everyone

Featuring Problems of All Difficulties

Capture the CTF will include all kinds of cyber security styled problems, such as cryptography, exploitation, reconnaissance, and reversing. We aim to provide a fun, learning environment for beginners while also hosting a competitive environment for well-seasoned hackers.
